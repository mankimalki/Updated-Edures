Frequently Asked Questions

How are tutors selected?

All our tutors are highly qualified professionals with verified teaching credentials and subject expertise. They undergo a rigorous selection process including background checks and teaching demonstrations.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel or reschedule sessions with at least 24 hours notice. Sessions cancelled with less notice may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Do you offer trial sessions?

Yes, we offer a 30-minute trial session for £10 which can be applied to your first package if you choose to continue.

How do online sessions work?

Sessions are conducted via secure video conferencing with interactive whiteboard technology. All you need is a computer or tablet with internet connection.

What if I'm not satisfied with my tutor?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your first session, we'll either match you with a different tutor or provide a full refund.
