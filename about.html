About EduResources

Empowering students through free, accessible education since 2023

Mr. Muhammad Ahsan Jahangir

Founder & Sole Benefactor

"Education should never be a privilege. By providing free resources to all students, we're not just helping individuals - we're investing in a brighter future for our society."

EduResources was personally funded by Mr. Jahangir with a vision to democratize education. As someone who faced educational barriers himself, he committed to creating a platform where financial constraints would never limit a student's potential.

Our Mission

In a world where educational resources are increasingly locked behind paywalls, EduResources stands as a beacon of free access. We provide:

  • Comprehensive study materials for all major qualifications
  • Curated past papers with model answers
  • Interactive learning tools and quizzes
  • Expert-developed syllabus guides

Unlike other platforms, we maintain zero advertisements and no premium paywalls. 100% of our funding comes from Mr. Jahangir's personal commitment and generous donations from supporters who believe in our mission.
